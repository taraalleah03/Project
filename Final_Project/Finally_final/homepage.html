Game Rules

  • You have 90 seconds per round.
  • You start with 3 lives.
  • Wrong answers cost a life, but you'll get a hint.
  • Choose which country to travel to next.
  • Find the best grass to win!

About the Game

Have you ever wondered if cows can fly? Meet our adventurous cow called 'Medium Rare'. She's escaping her farmer's barn, travelling the world in search of the world's best grass!

Solve puzzles, fly between countries, and help her stay ahead of the farmer. Cute, clever and surprisingly educational. It's the perfect escape from doom-scrolling!
